# Privacy Policy for KarmasQ

**Effective Date:** April 09, 2026

KarmasQ ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your data and is designed to comply with global privacy standards and Google Play's User Data policies.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Personal Information
To provide its core features (joining and managing queues), the App collects the following information during account creation and usage:
- **Full Name:** To identify you in the queue.
- **Email Address:** Used for account verification, login, and secure session management.
- **Phone Number (Optional):** Used to send SMS alerts or contact you regarding your turn (if applicable).
- **Profile Role:** Whether you use the app as a **Customer** or a **Business Owner**.

### Business Data (Owners Only)
If you register a business, we collect:
- **Business Name & Category:** To list your business in the search directory.
- **Business Address & Phone:** To help customers find and contact your establishment.
- **Services & Scheduling:** Data about the services you offer and your business hours.

### Queue Tracking Data
To facilitate real-time queueing, we process:
- **Queue Entries:** Token numbers, service types requested, and timestamps.
- **Queue Status:** Updates on whether you are "Waiting," "Serving," or "Completed."

---

## 2. Data Storage and Handling (Supabase Cloud)

**Cloud Storage:** Unlike strictly local apps, KarmasQ uses secure cloud infrastructure (powered by **Supabase**) to provide real-time synchronization between customers and business owners.
- **Data Hosting:** All personal and queue data is stored in encrypted databases.
- **Security:** We use Row-Level Security (RLS) to ensure that only authorized users can access specific records (e.g., only you can see your personal profile, and only owners can manage their business queues).

---

## 3. Purpose of Data Processing
We use the collected data solely to:
- Provide a real-time live monitor for your queue position.
- Facilitate the booking process between customers and businesses.
- Send turn alerts and notifications.
- Allow business owners to manage customer flow efficiently.
- Enable QR-code-based "Scan & Join" functionality.

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services
- **No Third-Party Selling:** We do not sell your personal information or queue data to any third parties for marketing purposes.
- **Information Visibility:** Your "Full Name" and "Token Number" are visible to the business owner of the queue you join so they can call you for service.
- **Infrastucture Providers:** We use Supabase (Database/Auth) to host our services. They process data strictly as per our instructions for the operation of the App.

---

## 5. Device Permissions and Their Use
KarmasQ requests the following permissions to function correctly:

| Permission | Purpose | Data Collection? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Notifications** | To send instant alerts when it is your turn or if a queue is delayed. | No |
| **Internet** | Essential for real-time syncing of queue data with our servers. | **Queue Data only.** |
| **Camera (Optional)** | For scanning QR codes at business locations to join queues instantly. | No images stored. |
| **Wake Lock** | To keep the Live Monitor active while you are watching your position. | No |

---

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion
- **Account Deletion:** You have the right to delete your account at any time. You can request account deletion by contacting support or via the profile settings in the app.
- **Queue History:** Queue entries are typically archived after completion for business analytics but do not remain "Active" once the session is over.
- **Manual Deletion:** Uninstalling the app does not automatically delete your cloud account. Please use the in-app deletion tools or contact us to wipe your data permanently.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy
KarmasQ is intended for general use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
